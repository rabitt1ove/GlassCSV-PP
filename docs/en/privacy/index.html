Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

GlassCSV does not collect any personal data. All file operations are processed locally on your Mac.

Data Collection

GlassCSV does not collect or transmit any user data. No personal information, file contents, usage data, or device information is ever sent externally.

Local Processing

All CSV files opened in GlassCSV are processed entirely on your Mac. File contents are never sent to any external server.

Analytics & Tracking

GlassCSV does not use any third-party analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Third-Party Data Sharing

Since no data is collected, there is no personal data to share, sell, or provide to third parties.

App Store Purchases

In-app purchases (Pro upgrade) are processed through the Apple App Store. Payment information is managed by Apple, and the developer of GlassCSV does not have access to this information. For details, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Since GlassCSV does not collect any data, no personal information from children is ever collected.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as needed. Any significant changes will be communicated on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about privacy, please reach out through our support page.
